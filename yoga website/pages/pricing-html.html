Location Considerations

Base pricing applies to locations within a 15-mile radius. For sessions beyond this area, a travel fee may apply based on distance:

  • 15-25 miles: $15 additional
  • 25-35 miles: $25 additional
  • Over 35 miles: Please inquire

Payment Options

To make yoga accessible to all, I offer several payment methods:

  • Credit/debit cards
  • Cash
  • Venmo or PayPal
  • Payment plans for packages (inquire for details)

Payment is due at the time of service for single sessions or upon booking for packages.

Cancellation Policy

I understand that life can be unpredictable. My cancellation policy is designed to be fair while respecting both our schedules:

  • 24+ hours notice: Full rescheduling or credit
  • 12-24 hours notice: 50% fee applies
  • Less than 12 hours: Full payment required
  • Emergencies will be handled on a case-by-case basis

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer discounts for ongoing weekly sessions?

Yes! For clients who commit to regular practice, I offer special rates for ongoing weekly or bi-weekly sessions. Please contact me to discuss a customized plan that works for your schedule and budget.

What if I need to share my package sessions with a family member?

Package sessions are generally intended for use by a single person to maintain consistency in your practice. However, I understand that flexibility is important. Please contact me if you'd like to discuss sharing a package with an immediate family member, and we can explore options.

Do you offer reduced rates for students or seniors?

Yes, I offer a 10% discount for students, seniors (65+), teachers, healthcare workers, and first responders. Please mention your eligibility when booking and bring appropriate ID to your first session.

What if I'm not sure which package is right for me?

I recommend starting with a single session to experience the practice and discuss your goals. After our initial session, I can provide a recommendation for a package that would best support your yoga journey based on your needs, schedule, and budget.

Do package sessions expire?

Yes, package sessions have an expiration date to encourage regular practice. The 4-session package expires after 2 months, the 8-session package after 4 months, and the 12-session package after 6 months. Extensions may be granted for special circumstances.
