Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking and sessions

What happens after I submit this form?

After receiving your inquiry, I'll contact you within 24 hours to discuss your needs in more detail. We'll talk about your goals, any health considerations, and find the best service and schedule for you. If you're ready to proceed, we'll confirm your first session date and I'll provide any preparation information you might need.

How much space do I need for a home session?

Ideally, you'll need enough space for a yoga mat (about 6 feet by 2 feet) plus some room around it to extend your arms fully. However, I'm experienced in adapting practices to various space constraints. Even a small area can work for a modified practice, and I'll help you make the most of the space you have available.

Do I need to have my own yoga equipment?

No, I provide all necessary equipment for our sessions, including mats, blocks, straps, and bolsters. If you have your own mat that you prefer to use, that's perfectly fine. For regular practitioners, I can also provide recommendations if you're interested in purchasing your own equipment over time.

How do I prepare for my first session?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows for movement. For home sessions, try to create a quiet space free from distractions. For outdoor sessions, dress appropriately for the weather. It's best to practice on an empty stomach (2-3 hours after a meal, 1 hour after a light snack). Most importantly, come with an open mind and be ready to communicate about what feels good for your body.
